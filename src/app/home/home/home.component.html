Chore Tracker

Create and manage chores for your whole family. Assign rewards to keep everyone motivated, and before you know it arguing about chores will be a thing of the past!

How it works

Parents start by creating an account for the whole family. Then each family member can have their own profile created with their own login. Parents can then add chores and assign them to one or more members of the family.

To keep everyone motivated, parents can add different types of rewards that can be earned by completing chores.
